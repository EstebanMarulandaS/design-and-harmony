What we do

Specialising in high-end interiors, Design and Harmony approaches every project as a distinctly individual journey. Here is an overview of our services:

  • Full exterior and interior design and installation service across new builds and renovations for residential and commercial projects
  • Selection of finishes and fittings, exterior and interior colours, flooring, hardware, kitchen and bathroom finishes, lighting and more
  • Furniture, soft furnishings, accessories, window treatments
  • Bespoke designed furniture
  • Product procurement and delivery
  • Art consultation and art commissions

01. Discovery

We begin by getting to know you. Our belief that design is ‘self-expression’ makes it essential to have a relaxed and open conversation around how you wish to live, work and celebrate within your space. This essential planning phase helps us establish the values that matter most to you. From here, we develop an aesthetic direction that is exciting, authentic and practical. By understanding your design vision, we can then review budgets and define the level of time involved to meet your requirements. We will then present an Estimated Fee Proposal for our services.

02. Concept

We now bring together a personalised catalogue, filled with inspiration and interpretations of your inside story. Your vision is further explored and the design footwork begins. This creative process is brought to fruition with spatial planning, design style considerations, initial finishes, imagery, product research and early concept sampling. We share how your vision will be brought to life as a ‘look and feel’ concept, a palette of colours, patterns, textures and materials that define the personality of the spaces.

03. Design

Following the creation of concepts and your approval, our curation, selection and procurement service begins. Working drawings are completed which provide detailed and dimensioned elevations for manufacture. Full specifications will include hardware, materials, accessories, and appliances if required. Schedules can include wall, cabinetry, flooring, fixtures and fittings, hardware, bespoke furniture, art and décor, and more.

04. Implementation

This is the ordering (or commissioning of the production) of furniture and furnishings, artwork and other items as required. Our knowledge of local and international products ensures each item is carefully priced and quotations presented. Our long standing relationships with proven industry suppliers provides you with significant value for investment. Bespoke furniture items can have lead times as long as 14 weeks, but we manage all product orders, communicate with key suppliers and coordinate deliveries to suit. We then select the final touches such as accessories and art as required. Every item is meticulously placed and styled on your behalf.
